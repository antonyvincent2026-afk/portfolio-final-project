About Me

Headshot of Antony Vincent, Credit: Kunduz Keldibekova

I am Antony Vincent, a journalism senior at Northwestern University with a minor in Business Institutions, and a certificate of Management in Creative Industries.

Raised in Doha, Qatar, I developed early training in accounting and business, which continues to shape how I approach reporting. At Northwestern's Medill School, I have built a hybrid skill set combining journalism, financial literacy, and market analysis.

My work focuses on companies, markets, and business models, explaining how financial decisions shape industries and real-world outcomes. I approach journalism with an entrepreneurial and analytical mindset, translating complex business and market dynamics into clear, actionable stories that inform decision-making. Currently, I am working on projects that explore the usage of AI in the media industry to enhance workflow efficiency.
